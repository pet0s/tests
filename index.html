subtextura is an immersive interface to peel back the surface and dive into the seductive logic of conspiracy. The phenomenon of conspiracy thinking has gained new momentum in 2025, transcending the boundaries of fringe belief and entering the realm of big politics. But what is conspiracy, really? Is it just the absurdity of logic and the falseness of facts, or does every conspiracy still carry a profound truth about society? After all, the use of metaphors often helps us begin with what might seem conspiratorial—then turn out to be true. Despite all criticism, the spread of conspiracy is psychologically justifiable—after trauma and disruptions, its adherents often seek refuge in the shadows of hegemonic ideology. It is a way to cope with real problems through imaginary means. But isn’t all mental activity balancing on the flickering line between truth and fiction, and using semantic extension as a tool? subtextura questions reality and the society we live in today.
